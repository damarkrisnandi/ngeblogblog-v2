---
title: 'Embedding Youtube Video'
description: 'Cara embed video youtube ke file mdx'
date: 'Tue Jul 09 2024 03:37:53 GMT+0000 (Coordinated Universal Time)'
tags: ["mdx", "youtube"]
---

```html
<iframe width={Math.floor(window.screen.width * 0.9)} height={Math.floor(window.screen.width * 0.6)}
src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/jfKfPfyJRdk" 
frameborder="0" 
allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" 
allowfullscreen></iframe>
```

ID ``fKfPfyJRdk`` dapat diganti dengan id lain. hasilnya adalah video embedding di bawah ini

<iframe width={Math.floor(window.screen.width * 0.7)} height={Math.floor(window.screen.width * 0.5)}
src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/jfKfPfyJRdk" 
frameborder="0" 
allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" 
allowfullscreen></iframe>
